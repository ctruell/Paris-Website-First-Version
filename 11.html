The 11th arrondissement is located on the right bank of the River Seine. It is one of the most densely populated parts of the city and recently one of the trendiest.

Famous Places: Cirque d'Hiver, Musée Édith Piaf

Places to eat: Le Chateaubriand (129 avenue Parmentier), Septime (80 rue de Charonne), Clown Bar (114 rue Amelot), Le Servan (32 rue Saint-Maur), Chez Aline (85 rue de la Roquette), Fulgurances (10 rue Alexandre Dumas)

Places to stay: Crowne Plaza Paris Republique (10 Place De La, Republique), Hotel Gabriel Paris (25 Rue Du Grand Prieure)
