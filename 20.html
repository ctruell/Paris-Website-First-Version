The 20th arrondissement of Paris, also known as arrondissement de Ménilmontant, is located on the right bank of the River Seine. It contains the neighborhoods Ménilmontant and Belleville. Famous people such as singer Édith Piaf are buried here.

Famous Places: Père Lachaise Cemetery

Places to eat: Le Baratin (3 Rue Jouye-Rouve), Popine (108 Boulevard de Ménilmontant)

Places to stay: Mama Shelter (109 rue de Bagnolet), Aparthotel Adagio Access Paris Philippe Auguste (12 Rue Pierre Bayle)
