4th Arrondissement

Home Famous Places Arrondissements Resources

The 4th arrondissement, also known as arrondissement de l'Hôtel de Ville, is located on the right bank of the River Seine next to the 1st, 3rd, 11th, 12th and 5th (on the other side of the Seine). It contains the southern part of Le Marais, also known as the gay district of Paris, and most of Île de la Cité.

Famous Places: Le Marais (shared with the 3rd arrondissement), The Centre Pompidou, The National Museum of Modern Art, Île Saint-Louis, Notre-Dame, Hôtel de Ville

Places to eat: Metropolitain (8 rue de Jouy), Le Bel Canto (72 Quai de l'Hôtel de ville), Georges (Place Georges Pompidou), Benoit (20 rue Saint Martin), La Caféothèque (52 rue l'Hôtel de Ville), L'Éclair de Génie (14 rue Pavée)

Places to stay: Hôtel de Josaphine Bonaparte (10 rue d'Ormesson), Hotel Duo Paris (11 Rue Du Temple), Hotel Bastille Speria (1 Rue De La Bastille)
