8th Arrondissement

Home Famous Places Arrondissements Resources

The 8th arrondissement is located on the right bank of the River Seine. It is one the main business districts of Paris formerly employing more people than any other arrondissement in Paris

Famous Places: Champs-Élysées, Arc de Triomphe, Place de la Concorde, Pont Alexandre III

Places to eat: Pierre Gagnire (6 rue Balzac), Le Grand Restaurant (7 rue d'Aguesseau)

Places to stay: The Hotel du Collectionneur Arc de Triomphe (51-57 Rue De Courcelles), Hotel Galileo Champs Elysee (54 Rue Galilee), West End Hotel Paris (7 Rue Clement Marot), Hotel Elysees Opera (17 Rue De Turin)
