The 15th arrondissement of Paris, also known as arrondissement de Vaugirard, is located on the left bank of the River Seine. It contains some of the famous neighborhood Montparnasse, which it shares with the 6th and 14th arrondissements.

Famous Places: Gare Montparnasse, Tour Monparnasse

Places to eat: L'Os à Moelle (3 rue Vasco de Gama), Le Grand Pan (20 rue Rosenwald)

Places to stay: Mercure Paris Centre Tour Eiffel (20 Rue Jean Rey), Aparthotel Adagio Paris Centre Tour Eiffel (14 Rue Du Theatre)
