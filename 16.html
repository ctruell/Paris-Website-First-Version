The 16th arrondissement of Paris, also known as arrondissement de Passy, is located on the left bank of the River Seine. It contains many museums. It is a favorite place of residence for the French high society.

Famous Places: Louis Vuitton Foundation, Passy

Places to eat: L'Astrance (4 rue Beethoven), Les Tablettes (16 rue avenue Bugeaud), Le Stella (133 avenue Victor Hugo)

Places to stay: Hotel Residence Foch (10 Rue Marbeau), Hotel Elysees Union (44 Rue De L'Amiral Hamelin)
