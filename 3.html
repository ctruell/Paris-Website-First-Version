3rd Arrondissement

Home Famous Places Arrondissements Resources

The 3rd arrondissement is located on the right bank of the River Seine near the city center. It is the 2nd smallest arrondissement by area and contains the northern part of Le Marais.

Famous Places: Le Marais (shared with the 4th arrondissement), Musée des Arts et Métiers

Places to eat: Elmer (30 rue Notre-Dame-de-Nazareth), Ambassade d'Auvergne (22 rue du Grenier-Saint-Lazare), Auberge Nicolas Flamel (51 rue de Montmorency), Breizh Café (109 rue Vieille du Temple)

Places to stay: Little Palac Hotel (4 Rue Salomon De Caus), Hotel Georgette (36 Rue Grenier Saint Lazare)
