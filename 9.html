9th Arrondissement

Home Famous Places Arrondissements Resources

The 9th arrondissement is located on the right bank of the River Seine. It is also a business center like the 2nd and the 8th arrondissements and has many places of historical and cultural interest.

Famous Places: Paris Opera House, Boulevard Haussmann

Places to eat: Helmut Newcake (28 rue Vignon), Le Bon Georges (45 rue Saint-Georges), Détour (15 rue de la Tour des Dames)

Places to stay: Hotel France Albion (11 Rue Notre Dame De Lorette), Hotel de Nell (9 Rue du Conservatoire)
