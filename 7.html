The 7th arrondissement of Paris is on the left bank of the River Seine. It is has some major touris attrictions such as the Eiffel Tower and also has many governement buildings.

Famous Places: Eiffel Tower, Hôtel des Invalides, French National Assembly, Musée Rodin, Musée d'Orsay

Places to eat: L'Atelier de Joël Robuchon (5 rue Montalembert), L'Arpège (84 rue de Varenne), Thoumieux (79 rue Saint Dominique), Restaurant David Toutain (29 rue Surcouf), Au Petit Tonneau (20 rue Surcouf), Divellec (18 rue Fabert)

Places to stay: Hotel du Cadran Tour Eiffel (10 Rue Du Champ De Mars), K+K Hotel Cayre (4 Boulevard Raspail)
