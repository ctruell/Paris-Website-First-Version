6th Arrondissement

Home Famous Places Arrondissements Resources

The 6th arrondissement is located on the left bank of the River Seine. It contains famous educational institutions as well as monuments and part of the Latin Quarter shared with the 5th arrondissement. It is Paris' most expensive area and also the richest.

Famous Places: Luxembourg Gardens, Pont des Arts, Pont Neuf

Places to eat: Ladurée (21 rue Bonaparte), Huîtrerie Régis (3 rue de Montfaucon), Ze Kitchen Galerie (4 rue des Grands-Augustins), Les Duex Magots (6 Place Sint-Germain des Prés), Café Trama (83 rue de Cherche Midi)

Places to stay: Odeon Hôtel Paris (3 Rue de l'Odeon), Citadines Saint-Germain-des-Pres (53 Ter Quai Des Grands Augustins)
