The 14th arrondissement of Paris, also known as arrondissement de l'Observatoire, is located on the left bank of the River Seine. It contains most of the famous neighborhood Montparnasse, which many American writes requented during the 1920's.

Famous Places: Gare Montparnasse, Montparnasse, Tour Monparnasse

Places to eat: Le Severo (8 rue des Plantes), L'Assiette (181 rue du Château)

Places to stay: 9hotel Montparnasse (76 Rue Raymond Losserand), Hotel Concorde Montparnasse (40 Rue Du Commandant Mouchotte)
