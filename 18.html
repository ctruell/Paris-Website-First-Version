The 18th arrondissement of Paris is located on the right bank of the River Seine. It contains Montmartre and the Goutte d'Or and African district.

Famous Places: Sacré-Cœur, Basilica of Sainte-Jeanne-d'Arc, Moulin Rouge, Montmartre

Places to eat: Supercoin (3 rue Baudelique), Lomi (3 ter Rue Marcadet)

Places to stay: Hotel Le Chat Noir (68 Boulevard De Clichy), Hotel des Arts - Montmartre Paris (5 Rue Tholoze)
