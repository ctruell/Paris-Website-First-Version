2nd Arrondissement

Home Famous Places Arrondissements Resources

The 2nd arrondissement is located on the right bank of the River Seine near the city center. It is the smallest arrondissement by area and is an important business district.

Famous Places: La Bibiliothèque Nationale de France, Opéra Comique, Paris Stock Exchange

Places to eat: Bistrot Vivienne (4 rue des Petits Champs) La Bourse et la Vie (12 rue Vivienne) A. Noste (6 bis rue du 4 Setpembre)

Places to stay: La Maison Favart (5 Rue Marivaux), Hotel Gramont Opera Paris (22 Rue Gramont), Golden Tulip Opera De Noailles (9 Rue de la Michodiere), Lyric Hotel Paris (2 Rue de Gramont)
