The 14th arrondissement of Paris is located on the right bank of the River Seine. It is devided into 4 areas Ternes, Monceau, Batignolles and Épinettes districts.

Famous Places: Place de Clichy, Palais des Congrè

Places to eat: La Félicité (148 rue Legendre), Coretta (151 bis rue Cardinet)

Places to stay: Hotel Cecilia Paris (11 Avenue Mac-Mahon), Monceau Wagram Hotel (7 Rue Rennequin)
